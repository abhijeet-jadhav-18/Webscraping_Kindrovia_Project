<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How Polo Training Foundation performs</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns">
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Expert Staff</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Coaching Credentials</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>The Polo Training Foundation employs coaches with USPA certifications and extensive experience in competitive polo. Many staff members have backgrounds in sports psychology and equestrian training, enhancing their teaching effectiveness.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Student Care</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Student Wellbeing</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>The Foundation offers a comprehensive wellness programme, including access to sports psychologists and nutritionists. Regular team-building activities foster a supportive community and enhance emotional resilience among students.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Curriculum</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Academic Integration</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>The curriculum includes equestrian management courses and sports leadership workshops, integrating academic learning with polo training. Students receive instruction in sports ethics and strategy, preparing them for diverse career paths.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->

</div>
