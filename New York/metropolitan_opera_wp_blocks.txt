<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How Metropolitan Opera performs</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns">
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Expert Staff</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Coaching Credentials</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>The Metropolitan Opera's staff includes Grammy-winning conductors and directors with degrees from Juilliard and Yale. Many have specialized training in vocal pedagogy and extensive experience on international opera stages.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Student Care</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Student Wellbeing</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>The Met offers comprehensive wellness programs, including access to performance psychologists and nutritionists. Regular workshops focus on stress management and building a supportive artistic community.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Curriculum</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Academic Integration</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>The Met partners with Juilliard for specialized opera courses, integrating music theory and performance practice. Students can also engage in masterclasses led by renowned visiting artists.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
</div>
