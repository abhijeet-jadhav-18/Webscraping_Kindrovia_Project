<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Our Assessment</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How American Ballet Theatre performs</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns">
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Expert Staff</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Coaching Credentials</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>American Ballet Theatre’s Summer Intensive has earned a reputation for being the most thorough and rewarding dance experience a student can have during the summer. Under the supervision of ABT’s Artistic Director Susan Jaffe and Artistic Director of Summer Intensives Kate Lydon, the program offers top quality teachers and master guest teachers that are dedicated to the learning process and growth of each individual student.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Student Care</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Student Wellbeing</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>Partnering with schools and local organizations, ABT provides an array of accessible opportunities to experience world-class repertory and artistry. Aligned with our mission to bring the magic of ballet to the widest audience possible, programs are designed to introduce the classical repertoire and a proficiency in movement vocabulary.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} --><h5 class="wp-block-heading">Curriculum</h5><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} --><h3 class="wp-block-heading">Academic Integration</h3><!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>American Ballet Theatre offers a rigorous curriculum that includes classical ballet technique, pointe, character dance, and contemporary dance. Students can also participate in specialized workshops focusing on choreography and stagecraft to enhance their artistic development.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
</div>
<!-- /wp:column -->
</div>
