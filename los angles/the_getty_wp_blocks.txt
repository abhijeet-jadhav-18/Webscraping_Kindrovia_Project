<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>Dozens of adaptable resources for teaching and learning about conservation and cultural heritage management Databases, publications, and more tools for general or specialized research and study Search for books, journals, auction catalogs, archives, photographs, and digital formats Highlights of our archival holdings containing rare source materials related to art history and visual culture Research and databases of archival documents, sales catalogs, and public collections Resources by topic from our library and special collections holdings Access to an extensive collection of digitized art history texts from a range of institutions Rare source materials related to art history and visual culture Customized reference assistance for the specialized Conservation Collection Bringing the work of this groundbreaking Southern California architect to the broader public Creating civic dialogue around urgent problems of our time by examining past and present connections between art and science Increasing recognition and improving conservation of African American heritage through more inclusive and equitable preservation practices Reconsidering Van Gogh’s Irises through material analysis and advanced imaging to understand its history and help ensure its preservation We are pleased to invite submissions to the Getty Research Journal, an open-access publication presenting peer-reviewed articles on the visual arts of all cultures, regions, and time periods.
